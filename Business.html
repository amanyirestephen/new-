— Our Story —

Who We Are

A Ugandan tech brand built on passion, integrity, and a deep love for premium electronics.

From a Small Shop to a Tech Movement

Steph Electronics was founded in 2018 by Stephen Amanyire in Kampala, Uganda, with a single display case and a dream: make world-class technology accessible to every Ugandan family, student, and professional — without compromising on authenticity or service quality.

We started with second-hand refurbished phones and today we are an authorized distributor for over 30 global brands, with 12 outlets across Kampala, Entebbe, and Gulu, serving over 15,000 loyal customers nationwide.

Every product on our shelves is verified, sealed, and tested by our in-house team before it reaches you. We believe technology should empower lives — and we take that mission seriously.

— Help Centre —

Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find your answer? Reach us on WhatsApp: +256783707912

Are all products 100% original and new? ▾
Yes, absolutely. Every product we sell is sourced directly from authorized manufacturers or their certified distributors. We carry zero grey-market or counterfeit products. All new devices come sealed in original packaging, and our certified-refurbished devices pass a rigorous 30-point quality inspection before sale.
How does your installment plan work? ▾
Our installment plans are available for products priced above UGX 400,000. You pay a deposit (10–25% depending on plan duration), then make equal monthly payments with 0% interest. You'll need a valid national ID or student ID. The device is yours from day one — no waiting. Plans available for 3, 6, 12, and 24 months.
What is your return and refund policy? ▾
We offer a 30-day return policy on all new products. If you're not satisfied for any reason, return the device in its original condition and packaging for a full refund or exchange — no questions asked. For faulty devices within the warranty period, we offer a free repair, replacement, or full refund. Refunds are processed within 3–5 business days.
Do you offer repair services for out-of-warranty devices? ▾
Yes! Our in-house repair hub handles screen replacements, battery swaps, charging port repairs, software fixes, water damage recovery, and more — for all major brands, whether you bought from us or not. We use only genuine parts and provide a 90-day warranty on all repairs. Walk in or book an appointment via WhatsApp.
How does same-day delivery work? ▾
Place your order before 2:00 PM and receive your device the same day within Kampala, Entebbe, Gulu, Mbarara, and Jinja. Orders placed after 2 PM are delivered the next morning. Same-day delivery is free for Steph Pro and Business subscribers. Standard customers pay a flat UGX 12,000 delivery fee. You'll receive real-time tracking via WhatsApp.
Can I trade in my old device for a new one? ▾
Yes! Bring in your old smartphone, laptop, or tablet for a free trade-in assessment. We'll evaluate its condition and offer you a fair trade-in value that can be applied toward any new purchase. Popular trade-in devices include iPhones, Samsung Galaxy, and MacBooks. You can also trade in accessories and peripherals.
What payment methods do you accept? ▾
We accept MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, Stanbic FlexiPay, Visa & Mastercard (debit/credit), bank transfers, and cash at all our physical locations. For corporate clients, we also accept purchase orders and invoice-based payments. All online transactions are encrypted and secure.
